1.1       Purpose and Scope:

 

The eDirham Instant mobile payment solution is a modern, integrated system for handling cardless transactions. eDirham Instant payment transaction is based on Token which is "One Time Password” (OTP). OTP is a unique identifier generated in the central system that acts as a substitute for a payment instrument. eDirham Instant mobile application will enable payments for government services where transactions can be carried out in e-commerce, POS, and directly in mobile application.

 

Simplicity, safety and speed are the features of the eDirham Instant mobile application. It is possible to pay either at a traditional brick-and-mortar merchant or online without the need to enter any sensitive data (for example: user’s name, card number or validity of the card). Possibility of fraud is minimized as all transactions are processed online with the customer personally approving transaction on his mobile device. Customers are also given an option to preapprove low value transactions.
